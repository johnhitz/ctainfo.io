Thing to See!

The Bean

201 E. Randolph st

The Bean is a sculpture located in AT&T Plaza done by Indian-born British artist Sir Anish Kappor. It was installed between 2004 and 2006.

Navy Pier

600 E Grand Ave

Navy Pier has been open to the public since 1916. The pier is 3,300-feet-long, and hosts parks, gardens, shops, restaurants, family attractions, and exhibition venues.

The Lincoln Park Zoo

2001 N. Clark St

Lincoln Park Zoo is a 35-acer zoo which opened in 1968. Enjoy viewing animals with breathtaking views of the Chicago sky line and period architecture.

The Lake Front

Chicago's water front in cludes access to the lake and parks along the 18.5-mile-long Lakefront Trail. Enjoy walking from Skokie to Hyde Park with access to the Field Musiem, the Shedd Aquarium, Lincoln Park, numerous marinas, and of course the lake. In addition to walking the trail is open to jogging, bicycling, and skating!
